Delete Your Data

Hushaby: Baby Sleep Sounds

Hushaby stores all your data locally on your device. 
No personal information is stored on our servers.

1. Delete your profile photo

Open Hushaby → go to Profile → click to cross button to remove photo → approve the action. 
The image will be permanently deleted from the app storage on your device.

2. Delete all app data

To delete all data stored by Hushaby (preferences, favorites, onboarding progress, 
and subscription state), simply uninstall the app from your device. 
All locally stored data will be removed automatically.

3. Subscription data

If you have an active subscription, uninstalling the app does not cancel it. 
To cancel your subscription, go to Google Play Store → Menu → Subscriptions → Hushaby → Cancel. 
Purchase history is managed by Google Play and RevenueCat in accordance with their privacy policies.

No server-side data to delete. 
Hushaby does not require account registration and does not store personal data on remote servers. 
Once you uninstall the app, your data is gone.
